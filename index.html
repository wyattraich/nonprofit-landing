Our Mission

At Fend, our mission is to protect the environment against the negative impacts of disposable consumerism. In times of global unrest, our goal is to reduce fast-retail practices by educating consumers and providing a mindful shopping experience.

We aim to bring awareness to the value of reusing goods & second-hand shopping by informing & empowering a global community. Our next goal is to explore innovative, green technologies to reverse the destruction of our planet.
